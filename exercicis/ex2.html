A group of energetic digital creators organized by Tsuneta Daiki (King Gnu), a producer and composer based in Tokyo, Japan.

Is a musical project that involves various artists and creators along with their own creative team "PERIMETRON". He is also a solo name providing music to other artists and groups. It's less of a solo side project and more of an audiovisual collective that Tsuneta describes as closer to art pop than King Gnu's more conventional rock stylings. Songs range from edgy, jazz-based electronic musings to thunderous synth mutations.

Millennium Parade, originally named Daiki Tsuneta Millennium Parade, allowed Tsuneta to explore sonic ideas rarely prioritized in Japanese pop, while showcasing the range of styles emerging in Tokyo.
