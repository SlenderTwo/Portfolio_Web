ABOUT THIS COLLECTIBLE FIGURE

Polyresin statue, size approx. 51 cm. Finished model, painted and numbered by hand. Limited edition. From the award-winning film made by Guillermo Del Toro, Warner Bros. Pictures and Legendary Pictures, the line of figures from the highest grossing film is now available! To combat the threat posed by the legions of monstrous creatures known as Kaiju, the world has developed a special type of weapon: huge robots, called the Jaeger. Sideshow Collectibles is proud to partner with the creators of Pacific Rim to present an impressive collection of statues. Gipsy Danger, The Titan of Steel, is the third Jaeger brand from the USA, equipped for close combat, and built to destroy any danger. Powered by a nuclear glowing heart, the Sideshow Collectibles Gipsy Danger statue was developed in collaboration with Guillermo Del Toro, handcrafted with painstaking attention to detail.
